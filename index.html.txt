  <!-- Case Study -->
  <section id="case-study" class="bg-light">
    <div class="container">
      <h2 class="section-title">Case Study</h2>
      <p class="section-subtitle">Real problem. Real solution. Real impact.</p>

      <div class="service-card p-5">
        <h3 class="fw-bold mb-4">Fixing Unbalanced Financial Entries Across Branches</h3>

        <h5 class="fw-bold">Problem</h5>
        <p>
          The system was generating financial entries where debit and credit were not balanced across branches. This caused reporting inconsistencies and required manual corrections from the finance team.
        </p>

        <h5 class="fw-bold mt-4">Solution</h5>
        <p>
          I analyzed the transaction structure and identified that cross-branch operations were missing intermediate accounting entries. I designed and implemented a solution using intermediate ledger accounts to automatically balance transactions.
        </p>

        <ul>
          <li>Added logic for branch-based balancing</li>
          <li>Introduced intermediate accounts (ledger 2128)</li>
          <li>Ensured debit = credit per branch and currency</li>
          <li>Automated entry generation using SQL and backend logic</li>
        </ul>

        <h5 class="fw-bold mt-4">Result</h5>
        <p>
          Eliminated over 90% of manual corrections, improved financial accuracy, and ensured all accounting entries are balanced automatically across branches.
        </p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>
